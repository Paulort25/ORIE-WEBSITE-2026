RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT
ORIE HOLDINGS

Innovation is not an afterthought at ORIE – it is embedded in our holding company’s constitution. Our MEMART grants us the power to conduct, fund, and commercialise research in any field, from semiconductors to space agriculture.

We believe that Africa’s future depends on homegrown solutions. That is why ORIE Holdings maintains a central R&D function that serves all subsidiaries – and explores entirely new industries.

Our R&D Focus Areas

🔋

1. Clean Energy & Storage

  • Next-generation solar cells and battery chemistries.
  • Hydrogen fuel cell pilot for heavy transport.
  • AI-optimised mini-grid management.

How We R&D

Unlike traditional corporate labs, ORIE’s R&D is:

  • ⚡
    Agile: Small, cross-functional teams with fast iteration.
  • 🤝
    Open: Collaborations with Nigerian & International universities and international research institutes.
  • 📈
    Commercial-focused: Every project has a clear path to revenue – either through a subsidiary or a new spin-out venture.

A. R&D Governance

Our R&D is overseen by a Scientific Advisory Board (to be formed in 2027) comprising academics, industry veterans, and the Group Head of QHSE. Projects are selected based on strategic fit, technical feasibility, and commercial potential.

B. Intellectual Property (IP) Strategy

All R&D outputs are owned by ORIE Holdings and protected through patents, trade secrets, or copyright – aligned with Nigerian and international IP laws. We also use open-source licences where collaboration is more valuable than exclusivity.
