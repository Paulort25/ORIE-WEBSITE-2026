The Backbone of a Modern Nigeria

ORIE Mobility, Energy & Engineering Ltd brings together three essential disciplines — mobility, clean energy, and engineering — into one powerhouse subsidiary. We build the systems that move people, light homes, and construct the future.

What We Do

Mobility

We provide transportation and fleet services that connect people and goods across Nigeria. From corporate fleet management and staff shuttle services to logistics and last-mile delivery, we keep Nigeria moving. Our fleet is managed to the highest standards of safety, reliability, and efficiency.

Energy

Nigeria's energy crisis demands bold solutions. ORIE Energy delivers solar installations, off-grid power systems, and energy distribution infrastructure for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. We are building a cleaner, more reliable energy future — one installation at a time.

Engineering

Our engineering arm provides civil, mechanical, and electrical engineering services — including design consultation, construction project management, and infrastructure development. We work with government, corporate, and private clients to deliver projects that meet international standards.
