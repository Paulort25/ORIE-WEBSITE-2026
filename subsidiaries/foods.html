From Farm. To Table. With Pride.

Nourishing Nigeria, One Meal at a Time

ORIE Foods Ltd is building Nigeria's food value chain from the ground up — sourcing quality produce, ensuring food safety, and delivering nutrition to homes, markets, and businesses across the country.

What We Do

Sourcing & Procurement

We work directly with Nigerian farmers and cooperatives to source fresh, quality produce — supporting local agriculture while ensuring consistent supply for our downstream operations.

Processing & Packaging

Our processing operations transform raw produce into consumer-ready products with strict adherence to NAFDAC standards, food safety protocols, and quality control procedures.

Distribution & Supply

We supply processed and packaged food products to retailers, wholesalers, hospitality businesses, and institutions. Our distribution network is designed for reliability, timeliness, and scale.

Retail & Consumer Products

ORIE Foods is developing its own line of branded consumer products that celebrate Nigerian flavours, ingredients, and culinary heritage — made to the highest standards for local and export markets.

Our Commitment

  • NAFDAC-compliant production at every stage
  • Support for Nigerian farmers and agribusiness
  • Affordable, nutritious food products accessible to all income levels
  • Reduction of food waste through efficient processing and distribution
  • Celebration of African culinary heritage in every product

Who We Serve

Retailers, supermarkets, hotels, restaurants, schools, hospitals, food banks, and individual consumers.
