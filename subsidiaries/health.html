Better Health. For Every Nigerian.

Transforming Healthcare from the Ground Up

ORIE Health Ltd is on a mission to make world-class healthcare accessible, affordable, and available to every Nigerian. Through diagnostics, pharmaceuticals, digital health platforms, and wellness services, we are reshaping what healthcare looks like in Africa.

What We Do

Diagnostics & Laboratory Services

State-of-the-art diagnostic facilities offering clinical laboratory tests, imaging services, and health screening programmes — accurate results, fast turnaround, trusted by clinicians and patients alike.

Pharmaceutical Distribution

We source, import, and distribute quality-assured, NAFDAC-certified pharmaceutical products to hospitals, pharmacies, clinics, and healthcare institutions across Nigeria. No counterfeits. No compromise.

Digital Health

Our digital health arm is developing telemedicine platforms, electronic health records systems, and health data analytics tools — bringing modern healthcare technology to patients and providers in Nigeria.

Wellness & Preventive Health

Prevention is better than cure. ORIE Health promotes corporate wellness programmes, community health outreach, immunisation support, and lifestyle health education.

Healthcare Facility Development

We partner with investors and government bodies to develop, equip, and manage modern healthcare facilities — from clinics to specialist centres.

Our Commitment

  • NAFDAC and SON-compliant pharmaceutical supply chain
  • No counterfeit products — ever
  • Affordable pricing models and health financing partnerships
  • Technology-first approach to healthcare delivery
  • Community outreach to underserved populations

Who We Serve

Individuals, families, corporate organisations (employee health), hospitals and clinics, pharmacies, NGOs, and government health agencies.
