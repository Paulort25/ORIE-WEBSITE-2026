Reaching Beyond the Horizon

ORIE Space Ltd is Nigeria's boldest aerospace venture — developing satellite technology, remote sensing capabilities, and space-based services that address Africa's most pressing challenges from orbit and beyond.

What We Do

Satellite Services

We are developing capabilities in satellite communication and data services — enabling connectivity, navigation, and information access in underserved regions across Nigeria and the African continent.

Remote Sensing & Geospatial Intelligence

Satellite-derived earth observation data for agriculture, urban planning, environmental monitoring, border security, oil & gas operations, and disaster response.

Aerospace Research & Development

ORIE Space invests in aerospace R&D — exploring propulsion, payload technology, and partnerships with global space agencies and private aerospace companies to develop Nigerian space capability.

Space Data Applications

We translate raw satellite data into actionable intelligence and applications for government agencies, private enterprises, NGOs, and academic institutions.

Capacity Building

Through internships, research partnerships, and educational programmes, ORIE Space is building Nigeria's next generation of aerospace engineers and scientists.

Our Vision

Nigeria has the talent. Africa has the need. Space has the answers.

We believe satellite technology is not a luxury — it is critical infrastructure for food security, national defence, environmental management, and economic development. ORIE Space exists to make that vision real.

Partners & Affiliations

We actively seek partnerships with NASRDA, international space agencies, aerospace universities, and private launch providers.
