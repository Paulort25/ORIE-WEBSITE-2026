About ORIE HOLDINGS

ORIE (Igbo for “great, principal”) is a Nigerian holding company with a vision to become the leading force shaping Africa’s future. We build and grow diversified businesses across essential sectors: mobility, energy, food, security, engineering, space, and health.

Our name reflects our ambition: to be the principal player in every industry we enter, driven by integrity, innovation, and long-term value creation.

🎯 Vision Statement

A future where ORIE is the standard for greatness: building sustainable industries, advancing human potential, and leading the continent’s transformation from the ground up.

📜 Mission Statement

To build and grow diversified businesses that deliver excellence, drive technological progress, and create lasting value – guided by the principle of ORÍE (greatness) in every venture.

Core Values

  • Greatness: We strive for excellence in all we do.
  • Integrity: We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and forward-thinking solutions.
  • African Pride: We are proudly Nigerian, with global ambitions.
  • Community: We build businesses that elevate the communities we serve.

Governance

ORIE HOLDINGS is governed by a robust MEMART that includes Class A super‑voting shares (held by a Living Trust), shareholder conduct policies, and strict compliance standards. The board ensures oversight, risk management, and alignment with our mission.

Sustainability at ORIE

We integrate environmental and social impact into every subsidiary:

  • Carbon reduction: ORIE Mobility prioritises EVs, ORIE Energy renewable sources.
  • Local employment: each subsidiary commits to hiring at least 30% of staff from host communities.
  • Governance: our MEMART enforces ethical conduct, anti‑corruption, and whistleblower protection.
